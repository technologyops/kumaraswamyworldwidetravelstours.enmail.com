Scenic Wonder New Zealand

New Zealand : 10 Days / 9 Nights

** Very Popular with Families, Groups & Couples from India, Japan, Europe, USA and Brazil **

Itinerary

Day 01 AUCKLAND

Welcome to New Zealand.

Meet & greet on arrival at Auckland International Airport and transfer to hotel.

Rest of the day at leisure, acclimatizing to New Zealand weather and enjoying Auckland evening life.

Hotels:

Deluxe (3-4 Star): Grand Chancellor Auckland City / Mercure Auckland or similar x 02 nights

Premium (4-5 Star): Carlton Hotel Auckland / Sky City or Similar x 02 nights

Day 02 AUCKLAND SIGHT SEEING

Full Breakfast at the hotel. Auckland Explorer Tour by luxury Coach, including Westhaven Marina, Harbour Bridge, American Express Viaduct, Harbour, Queen Street, Mount Eden, Parnell Village, Tamaki Drive, Mission Bay along with Kelly Tarlton's Antarctic Encounter and Underwater World.

In the afternoon enjoy on your own optional "Pride of Auckland Cruise" at Waitemata Harbour with Tea or Coffee and freshly baked muffins served on board. Cash Bar on Board

Day 03 AUCKLAND – WAITOMO – ROTORUA

Full Breakfast at the hotel.

Full Day Tour to Waitomo and Rotorua by luxury Coach with entertaining commentary. Today´s tour attractions also include a guided tour and boat ride through the famed and mystical Waitomo Glow worm Caves.

Arrival at Rotorua, the heartland of New Zealand, nature's spa of the South Pacific, host to some of the world's

most incredible earth forces. Evening at leisure and exploring Rotorua.

Hotels

Deluxe (3-4 Star): Rydges Rotorua / Lake Plaza Rotorua x 01 nights

Premium (4-5 Star): Royal Lakeside / Park Heritage x 01 nights

Day 04 ROTORUA THERMAL EXPERIENCE- QUEENSTOWN

Full Breakfast at the hotel.

Half Day sightseeing including Te Whakarewarewa Thermal Reserve, Geysers, boiling mud pools, hot springs and exploding geysers, including lively geysers which erupts daily up to 100 feet! Maori Arts and Crafts Institute guided tour - see Maori carving and weaving demonstrations, Rainbow Springs Nature Park, New Zealand Farm Show - an entertaining experience, giving an insight into New Zealand's industry and lifestyle.

Transfer to Rotorua airport.

Fly Rotorua / Queenstown (Airfare not included)

Transfer to hotel at Queenstown on arrival.

Hotels

Deluxe (3-4 Star): Novotel Gardens / Mercure Resort x 03 nights

Premium (4-5 Star): Millennium / Grand St. Moritz / Rydges x 03 nights

Day 05 QUEENSTOWN

Full Breakfast at the hotel.

Full Day at leisure enjoying Queenstown. Take a gondola ride in all the majestic views that Queenstown has to

offer or go shopping. There are dozens of activities including white water rafting, jet boating, bungy jumping, lake cruises, high country sheep station tours and excellent walking trails.

Day 06 QUEENSTOWN

Full Breakfast at the hotel.

A free day to enjoy Full Day Milford Sound optional Tour on your own. Enjoy an unforgettable journey from

Queenstown into Milford Sound by luxury Coach. Tour includes a breathtaking world famous 1 Hr 45 minutes

Luxury Scenic Cruise. Return to Queenstown in the evening.

Day 07 QUEENSTOWN – FRANZ JOSEF

Full Breakfast at the hotel.

Leave Queenstown and travel through the Kawarau Gorge via Lakes Wanaka and Hawea. Enjoy the rugged West Coast scenery through the Haast Pass and beech forests continue on to spectacular Fox Glacier and Franz Josef Glacier.

Hotels

Deluxe / Premium Punga Grove / Glenfirn / Alpine Glacier / Glacier Motel x 01 nights

Day 08 THE TRANZALPINE SAFARI

Breakfast at the hotel.

Enjoy the world famous The TranzAlpine Safari, a journey which is famous for its engineering feats and spectacular mountain scenery. The TranzAlpine Train (rated one of the top six train journeys in the World) runs from the Pacific Ocean to the Tasman Sea, crossing the fertile Canterbury plains and the stunning Waimakariri River. Arrive in Christchurch in the evening.

You would be received at Railway station and transfer to your Hotel at Christchurch.

Hotels

Deluxe (3-4 Star): Sudima / Scenic Circle Cotswold / Copthorne x 02 nights

Premium (4 Star): Grand Chancellor / Holiday Inn or similar x 02 nights

Day 09 CHRISTCHURCH – GARDEN CITY TOUR

Full Breakfast at the hotel.

The Garden City Tour highlights include Central City landmarks, Cathedral Square, Arts Centre, Botanical Gardens, Canterbury Museum and International Antarctic Centre to experience an Antarctic storm first hand, with temperatures dropping well below the zero... a great place to chill out.

Evening at leisure

Day 10 DEPART CHRISTCHURCH

Full Breakfast at the hotel.

Transfer from hotel to Airport with lifetime memories.

Depart New Zealand.

End of Services

at Rotorua, Queenstown and Franz Josef Glacier enabling the guests to enjoy more.
