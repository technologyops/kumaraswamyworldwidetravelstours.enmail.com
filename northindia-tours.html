HIMACHAL AT GLANCE

 

06 NIGHTS / 07 DAYS

 

(Delhi – Shimla – Manali – Chandigarh – Delhi)

 

Day 01:                   Delhi – Shimla

Arrival Delhi, Meet n greet at Rly Station or Aiport,  drive to Shimla, 8 hrs . Arrive Shimla in the evening. Rest of the day free at leisure. Overnight in the hotel.

 

Day 02:                   Shimla Sight Seeing.

After breakfast, enjoy local sight seeing of Shimla Kufri, Fagu, Jakhu Temple, evening free for mall road, Overnight in the hotel.

 

Day 03:                   Shimla – Manali

After breakfast, check out of the hotel and drive to Manali, 7 hrs. Arrive Manali in the evening and check into the hotel. Rest of the day free at leisure. Overnight in the hotel.

 

Day 04:                   Manali

After breakfast visit for local sight seeing, Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple, after lunch free for independent activities. Overnight in the hotel.

 

Day 05:                   Rohtang Pass excursion

After breakfast, enjoy excursion to Rohtang Pass - Situated at an altitude of 3979 meters above sea level offers a wide panoramic view of the mountains where you can also witness Snow. Later in the noon return to the hotel. Rest of the day free for own activities. Overnight in the hotel.

 

Day 06:                   Manali – Chandigarh

After breakfast, check out of the hotel and drive to Chandigarh. Arrive Chandigarh in the evening and check into the hotel. Later in the evening visit Sector 17 – one of the happening places in Chandigarh where you can enjoy nightlife. Overnight in the hotel.

 

Day 07:                   Delhi

After breakfast, check out of the hotel and visit Rock garden and Rose garden. Later drive to Delhi. Arrive Delhi and you are dropped at rail station.
