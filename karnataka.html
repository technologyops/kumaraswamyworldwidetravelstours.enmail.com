Tour 3.

Jain Circuit :

DAY ONE:
Start from Kundapur to Karkala, visit Gomateshwara. Proceed to Moodbidri. Visit Thousand Pillar Temple. After Lunch, visit Jain Mutts and other important places. Visit to Soans Farms, a world of agricultural and botanical diversity. Shop for spices and fruits. For more details visit  www.moodbidri.com/soans/index.html. Return to Kundapur.
DAY TWO:
 Visit to the ancient ruins of the lost city of the Pepper Queen which was a Jain Kingdom. Leave Kundapur at 7.00 a.m. Take a boat ride to the island. Visit the ruins of the old city. Visit Chaturmukha Basadi. Have a pure Jain Lunch in the Island. Start back at three in the afternoon. Stop over at Ottinene to see the sunset. Those who would like to swim in the Beach can do so. Back to Kundapur.

DAY THREE:
Visit to “HOMBUJA” Jain Temple. On the way stop at Shringeri or visit  JOGINAGUNDI waterfalls OR BARKANA (depending on the season). This is in the middle of the forest so we can not guarantee the visit to this place, as there are many chances of the way being blocked. After Lunch, proceed to Shravanabelagula. Visit Gomateshwara. Stay at Hassan.

DAY FOUR :
Visit Belur the world famous HOYSALA Architecture in the temples. If you decide to proceed to Bangalore, we can visit Hale Beedu or if we can leave before 12.30 noon from Balur, we can return to the railway station in Mangalore OR Return to Kundapur. Tour ends.
