                              7 night / 8 days - Itinerary for Mauritius

 Day One : Depart for exotic holiday in Mauritius, Today start your tour " ile Maurice" means island Mauritius, On your arrival in Mauritius you will greeted  by Pacific tour director, at SRG International airport, Mauritius. Transfers to Villa Mon Plaisir or Colonial Beach situated on North West Coast of
 Mauritius at pointe aux piments, on arrival welcome drink at your hotel rest of day is free for your, in evening enjoy your delicious Indian or your choice of   Veg/Non - Veg dinner. Overnight at Villa Mon Plaisir or Colonial Beach. (W,D)

 Day Two : We visit beautiful South of island, after delicious breakfast we take a tour to west & south of island, we start with Réduit, Mauritius University   Complex, Quatre bornes, Troux aux cerf (Valcano crater), Curepipe town, Grand Basin (Pari Talao), Hanuman temple, Mauritiuswarnath temple, Black
 river gorge, Allezander fall, Chamarel Cascade & Seven coloured earth, "Le Caudan" waterfront. In evening enjoy your delicious Indian or your choice of   Veg/ Non- veg dinner. Overnight at Villa Mon Plaisir or Colonial Beach. (B,D)

 Day Three : Your day at leisure, Optional Undersea Walk or Blue Safari Submarine Dive, after a delicious breakfast at your hotel, today the full day is  free, at leisure to explore this beautiful island on you own, You also have a option of taking Undersea walk or Blue Safari Submarine Dive, You can take a  walk under water and get a closer look at the fascinating marine life, even if you don't know how to swim! or Blue Safari Submarine dive 35 metre into   Ocean, during 40 Minutes dive, amongst multicoloured fish, you will discover the beauty of the corals and who knows what surprise awaits you on the  dive!.In evening enjoy your delicious Indian or your choice of Veg/ Non- veg dinner. Overnight at  Villa Mon Plaisir or Colonial Beach. (B,D)

 Day Four : See giant water lilies at Pamplemousses gardens, visit beautiful beach North of island, after a delicious breakfast at your hotel, we visit Port   Louis, Cita del. Thereafter famous Pamplemousses gardens, ranking among the finest botanical gardens in the world, are particularly famous for the giant   water lilies, a spice garden and the talipot plams, said to flower once every sixty years!.Thereafter Mount Choisy, Trou aux Biches, Grand Baie &   Super-U shopping mall etc. In evening enjoy your delicious Indian or your choice of Veg/ Non- veg dinner. Overnight at Villa Mon Plaisir or Colonial   Beach (B,D)

In evening enjoy your delicious Indian or your choice of Veg/ Non- veg dinner. Overnight at Villa Mon Plaisir or
 Colonial Beach. (B,D)
